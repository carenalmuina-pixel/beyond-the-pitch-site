Beyond the Pitch ("Beyond the Pitch," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through beyondthepitch.life (the "Site"), how we use it, and the choices you have. By using the Site, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Who We Are

Beyond the Pitch is a coaching platform for entrepreneurs — guidance, resources (including our book Nobody Told Me This and our free tools), and community support for people building real businesses. Contact us anytime at connect@beyondthepitch.life.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect what we need to run the Site and deliver what you sign up for.

Information you give us directly

  • Newsletter & free checklist opt-ins. When you sign up for our newsletter or download a free resource (like the Start Your Business checklist), we collect your name and email address. This is processed through Systeme.io, our email service provider.
  • Book & product purchases. When you buy Nobody Told Me This or another product, payment is processed by Stripe. We receive your name, email, billing details, and order information from Stripe; we do not see or store your full card number.
  • Community. Our membership community isn't live yet — the tiers shown on the Site today are a preview, and no account or payment is currently being collected for it. Once it launches, we'll update this section to describe what account information it collects.
  • Anything you send us directly — replying to an email, a contact form, or a direct message.

Information collected automatically

  • Standard technical data (IP address, browser type, pages visited, referring site) via our hosting provider's basic server logs.
  • Analytics and cookies: we don't currently use any analytics or tracking cookies. If that changes, we'll update this section and describe your opt-out options.

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13, and the Site is not directed at children.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Send the newsletter, deliver free resources, and respond to you
  • Process and fulfill purchases, including receipts and customer support
  • Operate, secure, and improve the Site
  • Meet legal, tax, and accounting obligations

We do not sell your personal information.

4. Who We Share It With

We share information only with the service providers that help us run Beyond the Pitch, and only as needed for them to do their job:

  • Systeme.io — email delivery and marketing automation
  • Stripe — payment processing (Stripe's Privacy Policy)
  • Our hosting provider, which serves the Site itself

We don't share your information with anyone else for their own marketing purposes, and we don't sell it.

5. Your Choices

  • Unsubscribe. Every marketing email includes an unsubscribe link. You can also email connect@beyondthepitch.life; we'll honor opt-out requests within 10 business days, consistent with the CAN-SPAM Act.
  • Access or deletion. Email connect@beyondthepitch.life to ask what information we hold about you or to request deletion. We'll respond within a reasonable time.
  • Cookies. We don't currently use tracking cookies or analytics, so there's nothing to opt out of today. If that changes, we'll describe the opt-out mechanism here.

6. Data Retention & Security

We keep your information only as long as we need it for the purposes above, or as required by law (for example, financial records). We rely on providers with their own strong security practices — Stripe is PCI-compliant, and Systeme.io secures email data in transit — but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll update the effective date above when we do. Continued use of the Site after changes means you accept the update.

8. Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Email connect@beyondthepitch.life.
